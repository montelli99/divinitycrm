 VA LOAN ADDENDUM TO PURCHASE AGREEMENT

This VA Loan Addendum (the "Addendum") is incorporated into and made a part of the Purchase Agreement dated [EFFECTIVE_DATE] by and between [SELLER_NAME] ("Seller") and [BUYER_NAME] ("Buyer") for the property located at [PROPERTY_ADDRESS].

1. EXISTING VA LOAN. The Parties acknowledge that the Existing Loan(s) secured by the Property is a loan guaranteed by the United States Department of Veterans Affairs ("VA Loan").

2. VA LOAN ELIGIBILITY IMPACT. Seller understands, acknowledges, and agrees that:
   (a) The VA Loan will NOT be paid off through closing and will remain as a lien against the Property after close of escrow;
   (b) The VA Loan remaining outstanding may negatively impact Seller's ability to obtain an additional loan through the VA in the future;
   (c) Seller's VA entitlement may remain tied up in this Property until the VA Loan is paid in full or assumed by a qualified veteran buyer;
   (d) Seller should consult with the VA or a VA-approved lender to understand the full impact on future VA loan eligibility.

3. ASSUMPTION POSSIBILITY. Buyer and Seller acknowledge that VA Loans may be assumable by qualified buyers. If the existing lender and VA permit assumption:
   (a) Buyer may attempt to formally assume the VA Loan, which would release Seller from liability;
   (b) Seller agrees to cooperate with any assumption process;
   (c) If assumption is not approved, the VA Loan remains in Seller's name and Seller remains liable.

4. DUE ON SALE RISK. The Parties acknowledge that transferring title to Buyer may trigger the due-on-sale clause in the VA Loan. If the lender calls the loan due:
   (a) Buyer will need to pay off, refinance, or restructure the loan;
   (b) Seller is not responsible for any costs associated with lender action;
   (c) Seller agrees to cooperate with Buyer in any lender negotiations.

5. CERTIFICATION. Seller certifies that Seller has been advised to consult with a VA-approved lender regarding the impact of this transaction on Seller's VA benefits and future loan eligibility.
